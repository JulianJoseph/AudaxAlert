All members of Audax (The Long Distance Cyclists' Association) will appreciate the satisfaction of completing a ride, and having their brevet card validated. To accompany that, for some, there is the added satisfaction of seeing your ride appear in the official Audax archive of results on their website.

Understandably, there is always a lag between completion of the event, and the result appearing on the website. This is where AudaxAlert comes in handy.

All you need to do is sign up with your Audax Membership Number (omitting any leading characters) and your email address. We will then monitor your results page at regular intervals and send you an email as soon as your latest ride appears.

If you are affiliated to an Audax Club, the service will also check your club results page and alert you when there is any new activity logged by a fellow member.

If you are interested in this free service, just go to the Sign Up page and register.
